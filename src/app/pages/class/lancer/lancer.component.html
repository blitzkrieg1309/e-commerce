Lancer Class

Class Lancer (クラス・ランサー, Ransā?), Knight of the Lance and Heroic Spirit of the Lance (槍兵の英霊?), is one of the Three Knight Classes among the seven standard Servant classes summoned for the Holy Grail War.

Qualifications

Servants placed within this class, many of which are Heroic Spirits who were knights, are very agile, display excellent close combat skills, show proficiency in hit-and-run tactics that capitalize on range and speed, and are skilled with long-range melee weapons such as spearWPs and lanceWPs. The qualifying conditions to be placed within the class are second only to the Saber class, requiring overall excellent attributes and a high agility attribute. Their only particular class ability is Magic Resistance, and although they "tend to be less flashy than the other classes", they display great reliability.
